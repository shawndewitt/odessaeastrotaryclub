About Our Club

Philosophy

The object of Rotary is to encourage and foster the ideal of service as a basis of worthy enterprise.

Duty

Rotarians gather weekly for breakfast, lunch, or dinner to fulfill their first guiding principle to develop friendships as an opportunity for service.

Programs

Rotary concentrates on promoting peace, improving health through disease prevention and treatment, improving the health of mothers and children, water and sanitation, education, and economic development.

Who We Are

Rotary is an international service organization whose stated purpose is to bring together business and professional leaders in order to provide humanitarian service and to advance goodwill and peace around the world.
